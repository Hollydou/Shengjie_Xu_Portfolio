1. Build an interface to DIY personal postcard (2016)

This is a web application to design postcard online, especially for young international travellers when they travel around the world. It only needs five simple steps to complete a postcard. Get the current location, choose a picture as the front side of postcards, choose another picture as stamp, type in greeting to people, and share the final postcard to social media or save it to timeline. It is also possible to use Myca in Chinese environment, since we provide multi-language options for those who may be not good at English.

The whole project took three months to complete, and the group consists of four members: Nan, Jim, Izzy, and me. In this project, my main works are list below:

  • Design and make paper prototyping materials
  • Give participants instructors in paper prototyping and digital prototyping user tests
  • Use Google Map APIs to get users' current Geo-location and transfer it to city name plus street name
  • Use HTML <canvas> tag to merge user's location, selected photos and a text into a front and back postcard
  • Display User's postcard in 'Preview area'
  • Use Facebook and Twitter's share button to share our website link to social media
  • Trade show demonstrator and the 2017 Innovation showcase student exhibitor
  • Produce reports

Code in Github: https://github.com/Hollydou/Myca

4. A website to address displaced People Settlement issues (2017)

Our client in this program was Kate Crawford of Eviva Consulting, Queensland Government’s Department of Science, Information Technology, Innovation and the Arts. She and her team in recent years have focussed on providing services and support for refugees. Displacement forecast to grow and immigration raise standard of living, however currently there lacks integrated service and the government support to resettle displaced people in rural area. Many refugees with Safe Haven Enterprise Visa(SHEV) visa would benefit from an integrated platform containing job seeking, accommodation, community service information for productive resettlement.

The object of this product is to develop a web-based platform to help displaced people to resettle productively by providing job, accommodation, education information and building a connection between offerors and refugees. This platform also contributes to enabling the organization to create, monitor and inform themselves about mutually productive settlement patterns and support for the asylum seeker in regional areas. In general, this platform is a website with two different dashboard types for offeror and refugees to achieve the core features of the product.

The whole project took half-year to complete, and the group consists of six members: Jiajing Sun, Yonglin Shen, Jinbo Shen, Izzy zhang, Bowen Li, and me. In this project, my main works are list below:

  • Display companies, courses, and accommodation's location on a leaflet map in different color
  • Write PHP to retrieve job, course, and accommodation datails from database
  • Search function: Use PHP to search for data that contains search keywords
  • Reorder function: Use PHP to reorder the data in current page depends on user's selection
  • Use PHP to display records in one page
  • Make profile page modifiable
  • Pagination
  • Produce reports
  • Build Information Website
  • Final product demonstrator

5. Designing a Web-based User Interfacer for Mapping and Analyzing Complex Journey to Work data (2017)

With the development of transportation system, people’s approach to travel to work become convenient and diversity.This phenomenon posed a big challenge to the transport institutions and policymakers who responsible for the urban planning and road construction management. Therefore, the visualization of the Journey To Work(JTW) data becomes an important element for them to acquire useful information. This project presents an interface includes four components (a circular plot model, two bar charts, and a map) to deliver detailed quantitative information on commuters’ travel path between accommodation and workplace in three years period 2001, 2006 and 2011 in Sydney, Melbourne, and South-East Queensland.

The main function of the circular plot model is to represent the linear route and distance between people’s accommodation and workplace. And the purpose of the Google Map API in this project is to display the real routes between two specified areas. we also implemented one bar chart to show the number of commuters using each mode of transport to get to work. And another bar chart to show the top 10 origins with the highest number of commuters.

This project was supported by the Urban Analytics Data Infrastructure (UADI), and undertook by the School of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering, University of Queensland. The whole project took one year, my supervisor Professor Jane Hunter instructed me to work with Dr.Tony Guohun.In this project, my main works are list below:

  • Design and use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery to implement an web-based interface to display the circular plot model, two bar charts, and a map
  • Use D3.js to build the static Circular Plot Model
  • Use D3.js to build two interactive bar charts
  • Use Google Map APIs to imbed a map
  • Produce reports
  • Final website demonstrator

Website link: http://uadi.project.uq.edu.au/UADI/index.jsp#

7. The 2017 GovHack competition (2017)

The GovHack competition hold every year is about encouraging and celebrating different technical and creative capacity with government for great outcomes, and building upon the social and economic value of Open Data published by government. My team name was "HealthOMeter". We evaluated the “customer-satisfaction with hospital” by using both Twitter data and the Government open data. Our project will help patients and the Brisbane City Council to understand customer satisfaction based on both Twitter data and the government open data. The objective of our idea was to enable patients and the BCC to reduce cost and find better clinic places to improve customer satisfaction. The highlights in this project was that we visualized the clinics' location and customers' location on a map. My main work is list below:

  • Build database to store Hospital's location information
  • Write PHP to work with database
  • Use Leaflet Map and .geojson data to build map
